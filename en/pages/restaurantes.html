Where to eat

Traditional parrilla, chef-driven cuisine paired with local wines, and tables among the vineyards.

Cafayate's dining scene has grown along with its wine tourism. From traditional Argentine parrilla to regional cuisine paired with local wines, here are some restaurants worth visiting.

Featured dining

Restaurants for memorable dining

Sponsor

Wine bar · Downtown

Bad Brothers Wine & Food Experience

Half a block from the plaza, with a menu by Chef Walter Leal — Le Cordon Bleu Paris, Académie Culinaire de France — a garden terrace and excellent music.

Visit → badbrotherswe.com

Restaurant · Winery

Amalaya Restaurant

Regional cuisine paired with the acclaimed Amalaya and Alluvión wines. A charming setting among the vineyards on the edge of town.

Visit → bodegaamalaya.com

Fine dining · Hotel

Aura at Piattelli Wine Resort

The restaurant at the Piattelli Wine Resort, offering gourmet cuisine in an intimate setting. Separate from the winery restaurant.

Visit → piattelliwineresort.com.ar

Lunch · La Estancia

The Clubhouse at La Estancia

Open to the public for lunch, featuring regional cuisine with golf course and mountain views. A perfect spot for a leisurely midday meal.

Visit → laestanciadecafayate.com

Fine dining · Hotel

Grace Cafayate Restaurant

Contemporary cuisine that blends regional flavors with modern techniques. An elegant space ideal for a special dinner.

Visit → gracecafayate.com

Restaurant · Winery

La Pausa de El Porvenir

Refined cuisine paired with the Laborum wines. A gastronomic experience among the vineyards.

Visit → elporvenirdecafayate.com

Chef-driven · Plaza

Pacha Cafayate

A fine dining experience by Chef Owner Tomás Casado, just off the main plaza. Refined regional cooking with local ingredients.

Visit → pachacocinadeautor.com

Restaurant · Winery

Piattelli Bodega Restaurant

Fine dining with panoramic vineyard views at Piattelli's winery. A unique culinary experience in a spectacular setting.

Visit → piattellivineyards.com

Regional cuisine · Hotel

Rosa at Patios de Cafayate

Regional cuisine and open-fire grilling in the courtyard of the historic boutique hotel just off the plaza. Colonial-era atmosphere paired with a local wine list.

Visit → patiosdecafayate.com
Local favorites

Casual restaurants and bars

  • 125 Güemes Bar and restaurant with music. Great for a relaxed evening in Cafayate.
  • Ampi Homestyle cooking in a relaxed atmosphere in the center of town.
  • Bacos A local hangout on the plaza, great for people-watching.
  • El Rancho Traditional Argentine parrilla on the main plaza, a Cafayate classic for barbecue lovers.

Cafayate has many more dining options around the main plaza. Want to recommend your restaurant? Contact us at info@cafayate.com.
