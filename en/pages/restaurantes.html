Restaurants in Cafayate

Cafayate's dining scene has grown along with its wine tourism. From traditional Argentine parrilla to regional cuisine paired with local wines, here are some restaurants worth visiting.

125 Güemes

Bar and restaurant with occasional live music. A great spot for a relaxed evening in Cafayate.

@guemes125

Bacos

A local hangout offering great people watching. A favorite stop for motorcycle tourists passing through Cafayate.

Bad Brothers Wine & Food Experience

The most complete dining and wine experience in Cafayate. Half a block from the plaza, the menu is curated by acclaimed Chef Walter Leal — a Le Cordon Bleu Paris graduate and member of the Académie Culinaire de France — complemented by a garden terrace and excellent music. Perfect for a relaxed evening with great wines.

www.badbrotherswe.com | @badbrotherswineexperience

The Clubhouse at La Estancia

Open to the public for lunch, featuring regional cuisine with golf course and mountain views. A perfect spot for a leisurely midday meal.

www.laestanciadecafayate.com

La Pausa de El Porvenir

Restaurant at El Porvenir winery, offering refined cuisine paired with their Laborum wines. A gastronomic experience among the vineyards.

www.elporvenirdecafayate.com

Piattelli Bodega Restaurant

Fine dining with panoramic vineyard views at Piattelli's winery. A unique culinary experience in a spectacular setting.

www.piattelli.com

El Rancho

Traditional Argentine parrilla on the main plaza, known for its grilled meats and regional dishes. A Cafayate classic for barbecue lovers.

Amalaya Restaurant

The restaurant at Bodega Amalaya offers regional cuisine paired with their acclaimed Amalaya and Alluvión wines. A charming setting among vineyards on the outskirts of Cafayate.

www.bodegaamalaya.com

Ampi

Restaurant and bar serving homestyle cooking in a relaxed atmosphere. A popular choice among locals and visitors looking for simple, well-prepared dishes in the center of Cafayate.

Aura at Piattelli Wine Resort

The restaurant at the Piattelli Wine Resort, offering gourmet cuisine in an intimate setting. An exclusive dining experience at the hotel, separate from the winery restaurant.

www.piattelliwineresort.com.ar

Grace Cafayate Restaurant

The restaurant at Grace Cafayate hotel, featuring contemporary cuisine that blends regional flavors with modern techniques. An elegant space ideal for a special dinner.

www.gracecafayate.com

Pacha Cafayate

A fine dining experience featuring the cuisine of Chef Owner Tomás Casado, just off the main plaza. Refined regional cooking with local ingredients in an elegant, intimate setting.

Cafayate has many more dining options around the main plaza and surrounding streets. Want to recommend your restaurant? Contact us at info@cafayate.com.
