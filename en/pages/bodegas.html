Wineries & tastings

Wine bars in town, historic bodegas, and extreme-altitude pioneers across the Calchaquí Valleys.

Cafayate offers an accessible and welcoming tasting experience. Most wineries are in town or on the outskirts, making it easy to visit them on foot or by bicycle. Between downtown wine bars and tasting rooms at the wineries themselves, there's an option for every palate.

Vineyards above 2,200 meters

Extreme altitude wines

At these elevations winemakers operate at the very edge of what's possible. Thin air, intense UV radiation, and 20°C day-to-night temperature swings push grapes to develop extraordinary concentration and complexity. These are some of the most sought-after wines in the world.
