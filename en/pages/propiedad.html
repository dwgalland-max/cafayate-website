Property

Houses, estates, land, and rentals in Cafayate and the Calchaquí Valleys.

Cafayate is becoming an increasingly popular destination not only for tourists but also for those looking to invest in property in a unique setting surrounded by vineyards and mountains.

Rentals

If you’re looking for a holiday rental in Cafayate, there are several options available, especially in La Estancia de Cafayate and the surrounding area. From houses with vineyard views to apartments in the town center. We recommend these two local rental agents:

  • cafayaterentals.com
  • cafayateholiday.com

Investing in Cafayate

The region offers investment opportunities in land, houses, estates, and tourism ventures. The growth of wine tourism has driven real estate development in the area.
